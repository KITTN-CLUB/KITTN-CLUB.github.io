Kittn Club

Everyone loves cats. Now you can remind your friends why. Every hour, of every day.
Kittn Club is a hourly SMS-based information service that provides subscribers (and their friends) with important information about everyone's favorite pet.

Everyone is curious about cats. Now you can help your friends learn, one helpful text message at a time!

Give the gift of happiness; simply add a phone number and we'll provide constant reminders of the wonderous feats of felines!

Don't let your friends be grumpy; sign them up for Kittn Club today!

That's cute, but how much?

Kittn Club will initially be free for all users, thanks to the generosity of its founders. However, a small fee might be required once membership grows.
